Organizations within Loghub represent a unique workspace for your company. 
If you need to manage events from different clients, SaaS applications, etc., you can create more than one organization. Each organization contains its own configuration and subscription plan. You can manage multiple projects within each organization.

### Create Organization
When you create your account for the first time, you will be able to view a list of organizations to which you belong or to which you have been invited to participate. In this case, you must create your first organization by clicking Add new organization
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/create_org.png" />
</Frame>

Next write the name of your organization
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/create-org-2.png" />
</Frame>

Now you must select the subscription that best suits your needs. If you need something special, do not hesitate to contact us.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/create-org-3.png" />
</Frame>

#### Stripe
The payment process will be carried out through stripe, a platform specialized in recurring payments.

### Result 
Once the subscription has been successfully completed you will be redirected to your new dashboard
